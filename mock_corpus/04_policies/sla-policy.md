# SLA Policy

## Standard support (Starter)

- Email support only, best-effort response during business hours.
- No guaranteed response time SLA.
- No uptime SLA commitment for Starter plans.

## Business support

- Standard business-hours support (Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm local time).
- Target first response time: 4 business hours for standard issues.
- Target first response time: 1 business hour for critical issues (service unavailable or data loss risk).
- Platform uptime target: 99.5% monthly.
- Planned maintenance windows communicated 48 hours in advance.

## Enterprise support

- Premium support with extended hours coverage.
- Target first response time: 1 business hour for standard issues.
- Target first response time: 15 minutes for critical issues.
- Platform uptime target: 99.9% monthly.
- Dedicated support contact available on request.
- Quarterly business reviews included.

## Custom SLA options

Custom SLA terms (e.g., sub-100ms API response time guarantees, 24/7 on-call support, specific uptime percentages above 99.9%) are available only on Enterprise and require a contract review and operations team approval before they can be committed. These terms cannot be promised verbally or in writing without that approval process.

API response time guarantees below 100ms are not part of any standard SLA tier. Customers requesting sub-100ms guarantees should be directed to Enterprise and the technical sales team for a scoping conversation.

## What the chatbot cannot promise

The chatbot cannot commit to SLA terms not listed in this document. Any request for a non-standard SLA — including specific uptime percentages, response times, data residency windows, or support coverage beyond what is listed above — must be escalated to the technical sales team or Account Executive for review.
