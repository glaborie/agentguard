# Plans and Pricing

## Starter

- **$29 per user per month**
- Monthly or annual billing available
- Minimum: 1 user
- Shared inbox (up to 3 mailboxes)
- Basic workflow automation
- Email support (best-effort, no SLA)
- 14-day self-serve free trial included
- No SAML SSO
- No audit logs
- No CRM integrations
- Self-serve setup; no guided onboarding

Starter is designed for small teams getting started with shared inbox and basic ticket routing. It is not suitable for organizations requiring SSO, audit logs, or CRM integrations.

## Business

- **$79 per user per month**
- Annual billing only (monthly plans not available)
- Minimum: 5 users
- Unlimited shared inboxes
- Advanced workflow automation (multi-step, conditional branching)
- CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- SAML SSO
- Audit logs
- Standard onboarding (guided, typically 2–4 weeks)
- Business-hours support with response time targets
- Platform uptime target: 99.5% monthly

Business is the most common choice for mid-market teams (50–500 users) that need integrations, SSO, and audit capabilities.

## Enterprise

- **Custom pricing** (negotiated based on seat count, modules, and contract terms)
- Annual contract required
- Minimum: 25 users (typically 200+)
- All Business features included
- Advanced security controls (custom RBAC, IP allowlisting)
- Custom SLA options (requires contract review and operations approval)
- Dedicated onboarding manager
- Solution design sessions
- Premium support with extended hours and faster response targets
- Platform uptime target: 99.9% monthly
- Quarterly business reviews

Enterprise pricing is not published. Quotes are prepared by the Account Executive once user count, plan requirements, and security/legal scope are confirmed.

## Trials

- **Starter**: 14-day self-serve trial, no credit card required.
- **Business**: Case-by-case. Business trials require sales qualification and AE involvement. There is no standard trial period.
- **Enterprise**: No standard trial. Proof of concept (POC) arrangements may be available for qualified prospects and require VP Sales approval.

## Billing notes

- Monthly plans are only available on Starter.
- Business and Enterprise require annual contracts.
- Annual contracts are non-refundable once active unless otherwise agreed in writing.
- Discounts apply only to annual contracts and require internal approval (see Discount Policy).
