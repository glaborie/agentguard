# Plans and Pricing

## Starter
- $29 per user per month
- Monthly or annual billing
- Shared inbox
- Basic workflows
- Email support
- No SSO
- No audit logs

## Business
- $79 per user per month
- Annual billing only
- Advanced workflows
- CRM integrations
- SAML SSO
- Audit logs
- Standard onboarding

## Enterprise
- Custom pricing
- Annual contract
- Premium support
- Dedicated onboarding
- Advanced security controls
- Custom SLA options
