# About NorthstarCRM

NorthstarCRM is a fictional B2B SaaS company that sells customer support and sales workflow software to organizations with 50 to 5,000 employees.

## Target customers
- Mid-market SaaS companies
- IT services firms
- E-commerce support teams
- Financial services operations teams

## Buying personas
- VP of Sales
- Head of Support
- Revenue Operations Manager
- IT Administrator
- Procurement Manager

## Value proposition
NorthstarCRM helps teams unify inbound support, sales qualification, routing, and automation in a single platform.
