# Technical FAQ

Q: Do you integrate with Salesforce?
A: Yes. Salesforce integration is available on Business and Enterprise plans. It provides bidirectional sync of contacts, accounts, and activities.

Q: Do you support SSO?
A: Yes. SAML SSO is available on Business and Enterprise plans. Starter does not include SSO.

Q: Do you support audit logs?
A: Yes. Audit logs are available on Business and Enterprise plans. They capture all user actions with timestamps and actor details.

Q: What are your uptime and response time SLAs?
A: Business plans have a 99.5% monthly uptime target and a 4-hour first-response target for standard issues. Enterprise plans have a 99.9% monthly uptime target and a 1-hour first-response target for standard issues, 15 minutes for critical issues. Custom SLA terms beyond these standards are available on Enterprise only and require contract review and operations approval.

Q: Can you guarantee sub-100ms API response times?
A: Sub-100ms API response time guarantees are not part of any standard SLA tier. If this is a hard requirement, it needs to go through the Enterprise technical sales team for a scoping conversation and contract review. We cannot commit to this without that process.

Q: Do you have live chat or real-time messaging?
A: No. NorthstarCRM does not include a native live chat widget or instant agent handoff feature. It is an asynchronous support and workflow platform. Teams requiring real-time live chat would need to integrate a separate live chat tool.

Q: What data retention do you offer?
A: Standard data retention is 2 years for all plans. Extended retention is available as an add-on on Enterprise plans.

Q: Is NorthstarCRM cloud-only or can it be deployed on-premises?
A: NorthstarCRM is cloud-hosted only. On-premises deployment is not supported on any plan.

Q: What certifications does NorthstarCRM hold?
A: Security certification details are shared under NDA during active evaluations. Contact your Account Executive to request documentation during an active deal cycle.
