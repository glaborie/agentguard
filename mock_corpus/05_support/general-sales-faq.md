# General Sales FAQ

Q: Can I talk to sales?
A: Yes. Qualified Business and Enterprise prospects can book a demo with sales. Starter prospects are encouraged to start with the self-serve trial.

Q: Do you offer trials?
A: Starter includes a 14-day self-serve free trial with no credit card required. Business trials are case by case and require sales qualification. Enterprise does not have a standard trial period; POC arrangements may be available for qualified prospects with VP Sales approval.

Q: Can I get a quote for 300 users?
A: Yes. Quotes are prepared once user count, plan, and requirements are confirmed. Contact your Account Executive or reach out via the website to request a quote.

Q: Can you match a competitor's pricing?
A: Requests to match competitor pricing require Deal Desk and VP Sales review. An Account Executive cannot approve a competitor price match unilaterally. This applies even if the competitor is offering a significantly lower per-seat price or bundled deal.

Q: Do you offer a discount for nonprofits or startups?
A: Discounts for nonprofits and startups are evaluated case by case and require Sales Director approval. They are not automatically available and are subject to the same approval tiers as other discounts.

Q: Can we start on Starter and upgrade later?
A: Yes. Customers can start on Starter and upgrade to Business or Enterprise as their needs grow. Upgrades are handled by the Account Executive and involve a new order form. Note that new-customer pricing applies only to new accounts; upgrade pricing follows the expansion pricing process.

Q: What is your minimum contract length?
A: Starter supports monthly contracts. Business and Enterprise require annual contracts. There are no month-to-month options for Business or Enterprise.

Q: Can we do a proof of concept before signing?
A: Proof of concept (POC) arrangements are evaluated on a case-by-case basis for Enterprise prospects. They require VP Sales approval and are not standard for Business or Starter plans.
